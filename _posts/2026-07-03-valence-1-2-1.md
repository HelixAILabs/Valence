Light themes just got the same visual delight dark themes have always had.

![Valence ink loading effects - the black-hole spiral drawn as colored-pencil ink on the Porcelain light theme]({{ '/assets/img/ink-effects.png' | relative_url }})

## What's new
- **Ink on paper.** On Porcelain, Bone &amp; Ink, Tide, and Dawn, every loading effect now paints as elegant ink on your theme's paper &mdash; hyperspace streaks become colored-pencil lines, the black hole becomes a hand-drawn vortex, Code Rain becomes typeset glyphs, and the CRT terminal becomes a typewriter log. No more dark box appearing over a light workspace while the AI thinks. Dark themes are untouched &mdash; same glow as always.
- **Every effect, every theme.** All 16 effects (plus the classic spiral backdrop) adapt automatically &mdash; nothing was removed, and the live preview in Settings &rarr; Appearance shows exactly how each one looks on your current theme.

## Fixes
- Local models no longer occasionally repeat their own instructions instead of answering.
- If a reply quotes ability syntax in passing, it now stays on screen as normal text instead of vanishing into a failed tool call.
