Valence is here — one private, controlled, curated space for every AI model you use. Thanks for being part of the first release.

## What you can do

- **Chat across providers** — OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Azure, xAI, OpenRouter, Ollama, and on-device local models — from one interface.
- **Run fully local** — download a model (Gemma, Qwen) and chat with nothing leaving your PC.
- **Give Valence a memory** — it remembers what matters across conversations, on your machine.
- **Bring your own documents** — attach files and let Valence answer from them (per-profile knowledge).
- **Compare models side by side**, including your local ones.
- **Peek under the hood with X-Ray** — see, in plain English, why the AI answered the way it did.

## For families

Kids Mode pairs a multi-layer safety system with a parent dashboard: scan conversations, get crisis alerts, review what needs attention, and set daily time limits — all on-device.

## Private by design

Your conversations, memory, and documents stay on your computer. No cloud account required.
