The AI Providers page went from a wall of cards to a calm, precise control room.

![Valence AI Providers - provider chips with status dots and the Local AI card]({{ '/assets/img/provider-chips.png' | relative_url }})

## What's new

- **Pick a provider, see its card.** One chip per provider across the top &mdash; with a live status dot showing what's ready &mdash; and exactly one provider's settings open below. Local AI is front and center; any cloud provider is one click away. Drag the cloud chips to reorder them.
- **The model library is a table now.** Every local model on one line &mdash; name, size, and exactly how much RAM it needs &mdash; instead of paragraph-sized cards. Models your machine can't comfortably run are dimmed with a clear warning. Hover any model name for its description.
- **Gemma vs Qwen at a glance.** The family guidance is a three-row comparison table &mdash; best for, character, trade-off &mdash; instead of prose you had to read twice.
- **Cloud Sampling knows where you are.** The advanced sampling panel now appears only for cloud providers and arrives pre-scoped to the provider you selected.

## Fixes

- The last of the old diamond-era artwork is gone &mdash; the trinity-knot mark now appears everywhere, including the About page and trial screen.
