Our biggest release yet: a real step forward for families, and a calmer, more honest everyday experience - still private and on your machine.

## What's new

**Kids privacy &amp; safety**

- **Locked down by default.** Kids Mode is now deny-by-default - a child can only reach what a parent has explicitly opened.
- **Real help in a crisis.** If a child in distress mentions self-harm, Valence reliably surfaces the 988 Suicide &amp; Crisis Lifeline rather than trying to handle it alone.
- **Private, even on the device.** Conversations can now be encrypted at rest - locked on disk so no one else with access to the computer can read them. A new Local-Only mode blocks all network egress, so nothing leaves the machine.
- **A parent console** to review a child's activity and emotional patterns at a glance, with the moments that matter surfaced first.

**A calmer, more honest AI**

- **Calm streaming (new default).** While the model works you see a quiet "responding" state; the finished reply appears once, cleanly formatted - no jittery live text. Want to watch it stream? One click.
- **Your AI, by name** (like "Qwen 3 - Medium (Fast)"), not a cryptic ID.
- **Honest first run** - no claiming a graphics card you don't have; on a slower machine it tells you plainly while the first model warms up, then answers the moment it's ready.
- **Tidier thinking** - the Chain of Thought is a small, collapsible note instead of a wall of text.
- **Quieter by default** - optional tools don't connect on their own.

## Fixes
- Sharper, more relevant memory.
- Fixed a rare crash on very long replies, a greeting quirk on small local models, and a case where a follow-up reply could land in the previous message's space.
