Your memories now open in a spacious, focused window instead of a cramped pop-up.

## What's new
- **A real Memory window.** Browsing your memories now opens an anchored window &mdash; the same calm, glassy surface as the Conversation Explorer &mdash; with an at-a-glance stats strip (memories, messages, topics, last updated) and an activity sparkline.
- **Filter the way you think.** A left rail lets you jump to All, Recent, or any of your top topics in one click, alongside live search, sorting, and a precise date range.
- **Edit in a slide-in panel.** Click any memory and an editor glides in from the right &mdash; rename it, polish the summary, add or remove topics, and read back the conversation &mdash; without losing your place in the list.
- **Tidier and clearer.** Cleaner cards, multi-select for bulk cleanup, and a one-tap Import menu for ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini history.

## Fixes
- Selecting **Local Model** before downloading a model no longer shows a confusing "no API key" error &mdash; it now points you to download a model instead.
