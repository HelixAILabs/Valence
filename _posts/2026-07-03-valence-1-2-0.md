How the AI's words appear is now yours to choose &mdash; the streaming effects we previewed last week are here.

![Valence Streaming Reveal - Weight Settle landing hot and cooling mid-reply]({{ '/assets/img/streaming-reveal.png' | relative_url }})

## What's new
- **Streaming Reveal.** Pick the style replies use as they stream in, under **Settings &rarr; Theme &rarr; Streaming Reveal**: **None** keeps today's instant text (still the default), **Focus Pull** racks each word into focus from a soft blur, **Weight Settle** lands every character hot and heavy before it cools into place, and **Line Rise** gathers words into lines that rise into view. A live preview plays each style before you commit, and the finished reply always settles into the same polished formatting as before.
- **Updates find you.** Checking for updates on startup is now on by default, so new versions announce themselves &mdash; you can turn it off any time in Settings.

## Fixes
- Keyboard users can no longer Tab onto invisible menu items while the menu's "More options" section is collapsed.
