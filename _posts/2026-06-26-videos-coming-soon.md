We're putting together a set of short videos to make Valence easier to get started with — and to show what it's really for.

## On the way

- **An installation walkthrough** — from download to your first local model, step by step.
- **How-to videos** — quick guides to the features people ask about most: switching models, local memory, knowledge files from your own documents, model compare, X-Ray, and Kids Mode.
- **A few product videos** that get at *why* Valence exists:
  - **Shrink your subscription footprint.** Instead of paying monthly for several separate AI services, Valence is a one-time purchase that brings your own keys — or fully local, on-device models — together in one place. Less recurring spend, more control over what you're paying for.
  - **A safer place for children to learn and use AI.** Kids Mode is a locked-down, on-device space with layered safety and a parent dashboard — a calmer, more accountable way for kids to explore AI, with you in the loop.
