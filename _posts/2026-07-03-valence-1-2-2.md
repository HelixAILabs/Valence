Yesterday's ink got a wardrobe, and Settings learned to stay out of your way.

![Valence theme-matched ink effects - the hyperspace effect drawn in warm sepia ink on the Bone &amp; Ink light theme]({{ '/assets/img/ink-themes.png' | relative_url }})

## What's new
- **Every light theme gets its own ink.** Loading effects now draw in colors matched to your theme &mdash; indigo on Porcelain, warm sepia on Bone &amp; Ink, ocean blues on Tide, sunrise coral on Dawn. The live preview in Settings shows exactly what you'll get, and switching themes swaps the ink instantly.
- **Settings, reorganized.** Long pages split into quick views you pick with chips &mdash; Theme (Style / Motion / Text &amp; Input / Window), Local Model, and About &mdash; with everything inside already open. No more clicking section after section to find things. On smaller windows the navigation collapses into one compact header instead of pushing content off screen.
- **Growing Tree, tamed.** The blossom canopy reads as blossoms over branches instead of one glowing mass &mdash; on every theme.

## Fixes
- Appearance controls (compact mode, opacity, always-on-top) no longer stop responding after revisiting the Theme tab.
