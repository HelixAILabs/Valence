A stability release focused on the installer.

Earlier builds shipped an **incomplete** set of app assets inside the MSI — some interface and theme files were missing, which could leave parts of a fresh install looking or behaving wrong. 1.0.1 rebuilds the installer so it **auto-harvests and bundles the complete asset tree**, and adds a packaging-completeness check to the build so it can't regress.

If you're on 1.0.0, updating to 1.0.1 (or straight to the latest) is recommended — installs upgrade in place and keep your settings, profiles, and conversations.
